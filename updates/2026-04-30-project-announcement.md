# We're off!

Consider this a 'soft launch'. We're currently reaching out to the climbing communities that care for and about Chomolungma. We're starting to gather imagery shared in public, and we're building our research infrastructure to manage and study these materials. We hope to hire some student colleagues soon. 

Below is one of the earliest depictions of Chomolungma on film. It was posted to Youtube by user [The Unfinished Self](https://www.youtube.com/@The_Unfinished_Self). At the end, some magic lantern slides are reproduced. (We would be delighted to hear from anyone who might know of similar early materials concerning the mountain!)
